Why I Wrote It

I've spent the last stretch as a staff engineer shipping real features to production multiple times a day with AI in the loop. Not slop—actual, reviewed, maintainable code that moves product forward. This ebook distills what works in practice so you can get results fast without sacrificing quality.

What You’ll Learn

It’s a concise, hands‑on guide to an AI‑first workflow: define outcomes, set constraints, ask for a plan, implement in short loops, and verify relentlessly. You’ll get concrete patterns, acceptance‑criteria habits, and tactics for keeping momentum while maintaining standards.

  • Run the conversation loop: goal → plan → implement → verify → iterate.
  • Write acceptance criteria a human can check in 30 seconds.
  • Ship in short, visible steps without losing quality.
  • Steer with constraints for performance, usability, and robustness.
  • Keep a readable history that explains intent, not just changes.
